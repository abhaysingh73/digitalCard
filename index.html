About Us -

At Anshika Consultancy, we're all about helping businesses grow and succeed. Based in Mumbai, Maharashtra, we’re a passionate team dedicated to providing practical solutions that make a real impact.
We specialize in sales consulting, helping companies boost their sales, fine-tune strategies, and achieve sustainable growth. We know that every business is unique, so we take the time to understand your challenges and offer personalized approaches that work best for you.
Alongside sales consulting, we offer innovative software solutions like inventory management systems and sales management tools that simplify your operations and improve efficiency. And, as the world goes digital, we provide Digital Cards and Digital Marketing services to help you modernize your branding and connect with your audience in meaningful ways.
At Anshika Consultancy, our goal is simple: to support businesses of all sizes with the right tools, insights, and strategies to thrive in a fast-changing world. Whether you’re just starting out or looking to grow, we’re here to help you take your business to the next level.
Let’s work together to make your vision a reality!
